Ryan Bush

Hello, my name is Ryan Bush and I am a student at UGA. I was born in Thomasville, GA on September 12, 1996. I grew up in Thomasville, but moved to Pelham when I was 15 which is only about 20 minutes away. Not many people know about Thomasville or Pelham, they are just small towns in South Georgia with absolutely nothing to do, but count the days as they go by. Coming to Athens, GA was not a tough transition, but was something I had never experienced and was more eager than I have ever been to embark on starting somewhere totally different. Athens is by no means a large city but when you come from a town that has two stop lights and if you blink about four times you are already out of the city limits, it is quite the change. Especially if the change is graduating from a high school with only 500 people, and most of my lecture classes have 300 people which is two times the size of my graduating class. With the University of Georgia having roughly 35,000 students and Thomasville’s entire city population being about 18,000. Pelham’s population is only 3,841. I am thankful though for getting to experience such a great university and change in my life. Hobbies
